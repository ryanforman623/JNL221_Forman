This data is all about the earnings in sports contracts. This data is organized by overall earnings per year and organized by sport. Sports contracts are only going up and it’s interesting to compare from 8 to 10 years ago and just see that contracts have only gone up. My hypothesis would be: if you’re in the BIG 10 conference, than you’re probably getting the biggest bonus.
