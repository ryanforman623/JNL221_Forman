Syracuse University, Fall 2025

Looking ahead, the Bronx will see more than 1,000 tree plantings in the December 2025 season, an almost 50% increase from the privious planting season. In the January 2026 planting season, there are no tree plantings planned for the Manhattan or Staten Island boroughs, but over 2,500 plantings for Queens. Of the six plantings planned for Manhattan in the June 2026 planting season, two are on West End Avenue and two are on Columbus Avenue, all are in the 6th district.
